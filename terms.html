Acquisition

  • Shawlkits may be acquired in a purchase via Shawlkit items or currency, or via PCE items, cats, or currency.
  • Shawlkits may be traded to other users in exchange for Shawlkits, Shawlkit currency or items, or PCE cats, currency or items, or art/writing.
  • Shawlkits may be gifted to other users.
  • Shawlkits may not be traded, gifted, or sold in exchange for real-world currency. (This may change, but I am uncomfortable with it.)

Ownership

  • Co-Ownership is not currently allowed, it makes the masterlist really complicated.
  • Upon gaining ownership of a Shawlkit, you may do pretty much whatever you would like with it, as long as that does not include anything NSFW or illegal.

Ownership Transfers

  • To transfer a Shawlkit, both users must consent to the terms and payment, and then inform an admin
  • Upon trading, reselling or gifting the design to another individual, the previous owner agrees to transfer all ownership rights to the recipient.

Summons

Summoning Items may be acquired through several means:

  • Item Shop
  • Crafting via resources
  • Trading with other players
  • Events

After a summoning item is acquired, a dummy slot will be added to the masterlist to keep track of it.

Summon Designs

  • When summoning a Shawlkit using tokens, a random design will be selected from the Design Bank, with a rarity depending on the token you use.
  • A design summoned using a Blank Cloak may be created by the user (you!)
    • Designs created via Blank Cloaks must follow the species guidelines and also the creation guidelines: Creation Guidelines
    • A design must be visually distinct from existing designs.
    • A design must not include hate speech or offensive/inappropriate materials.
    • A design must only include traits up to the rarity allowed in the summoning item.
  • To submit your design, please go to the Design Submission channel in the discord!

Trait Edits

  • When adding a trait or addon to a design, please keep the design otherwise the same.
